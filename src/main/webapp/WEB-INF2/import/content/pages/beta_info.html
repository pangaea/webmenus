About Our FREE Beta Program


We are pleased to offer chowMagic (online restaurant menu management and order taking) free of charge. No credit card is required. No salesman will call you.


You can signup and make an account in minutes. Use this service to present your restaurant's menus online and take orders via email (more options to come). You can choose to receive orders as PDF attachments and use any popular FAXing service to send them to a FAX machine.


No software or hardware to buy, everything is in the cloud.
